REFLECTION OF EACH COURSES

SUMMARISATION

SECI1013-DISCRETE STRUCTURE

This course introduces to the principles and applications of discrete structure in field of computer sciece. There are 5 topics that covered and should understand and apply those topics to solve problems which are set theory and logic, relations and functions,counting and probability, graph theory and finite automata.One of the challenges that I faced was to understanding and how to apply the counting and probability concepts as I find it quite confusing to interpret the problem statements and unsure if my labeling to solve the questions were correct or not.

SECR1013-DIGITAL LOGIC

Digital logic introduces students to digital electronic and stay up-to-date of digital fundamentals which covered 9 chapters. Not only through deeds simulator application, student also gets to experience to stimulate the actual circuit through hands-one lab activities. I'm able to draw the theoretical flip-flop circuit but find it difficult to create and simulate the circuit into the deeds application.

SECJ1013-PROGRAMMING TECHNIQUE 1

This course provides students with both theory and practice on problem solving tehcnique by developing program using C++ programming languange.There are 8 chapters covered in this course.I'm still struggling with pointer chapter as it involve working with unique memory locations.Sometimes I'm still confused on how to differentiate between pointer variables and indirection operator.I'm also mix up array and pointer concepts as both involve in memory addressing and data access.

SECP1513-TECHNOLOGY AND INFORMATION SYSTEM

This course introduces students to information system and technology (IS/IT), covering topics such as privacy,security and ethic, databases and data analytic,cloud computing and more.8 chapters,with students presenting for each topic from each chapter which helps to develop the communication and teamworking skills. However, there are so many terms to understand and remember as trends in IT/IS are always changing.

ULRS1032-INTERGRITY AND ANTI-CORRUPTION

This special course introduced students the concepts on importance of core value, practicing student's intregrity as well as understand the concept of corruption and its prevention in society and organziation.This class was very interesting.Student gains knowledge as we have to do report for corruption case that was assigned for each group.We also get the opportunity to create our own intregrity and anti-corrupsion video as part of our assesment for this course.
